Who Am I?

Hi I'm Viren Khandal I’m currently a second-year student at the University of California, Berkeley with strong experience in computer science and various leadership positions. Outgoing and detail-oriented, I am proficient in app and software development, via a multitude of coding languages. My diversified skill set covering business administration, artificial intelligence, and several sciences is evident in my work. During my junior year, I attended the 2018 AWS Summit and took part in a professional Hackathon, in which I placed 3rd. This is where my passion for computer science took off. I then went on and worked with a plethora of AWS employees on various coding projects. This assured me that computer science was the right career path.

My interests outside of computer science include business administration and finance. Having won at DECA’s International Business Competition for two years in a row, I decided to combine all my knowledge to become a Student Outreach Manager at Wells Fargo and a few other banks.
