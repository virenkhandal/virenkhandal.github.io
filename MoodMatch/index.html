Our Process

A simple idea brought to life

What started off as a solution to a common issue faced by millions of people throughout the world, MoodMatch has grown into a lot more than just that.

Inspiration

The simple problem of not knowing what to listen to or what to watch inspired us to create MoodMatch.

Design

What started off as a Facebook Messenger bot is now compatible on Amazon Alexa and Google Home Mini.

Development

Heavily based in Python and JavaScript, MoodMatch is the epitome of an end-to-end software pipeline
